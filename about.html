J. Maree

I help people reconnect with themselves through clarity, storytelling, and practices that center emotional and creative intelligence.

My work isn’t about quick fixes or formulas. It’s about creating space to explore what’s alive in you right now. Through storytelling, voice-guided rituals, and carefully designed practices, I help people reconnect with themselves—and trust what they feel, think, and desire.

I’ve worn a few hats: yoga teacher, community health coach, brand storyteller, and lifelong seeker of what makes life feel meaningful. Through each path, I’ve realized the same thing: the simplest, quietest practices—applied with attention—can restore clarity, confidence, and creativity. People don’t need more noise. They need space.

Space to feel.

Space to reflect.

Space to hear themselves clearly.

My Approach

I create thoughtful conditions for reconnection through storytelling, voice, ritual, and attention. Whether through personal practices or creative collaboration, I focus on clarity, emotional intelligence, and grounded presence.
